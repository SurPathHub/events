Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is #100DaysOfCode challenge?

A. The 100 Days Of Code challenge encouraging beginners to start to learn code, encourage them to post a tweet about what they did and use the hashtag of #100DaysOfCode and submit the final project after the event into showcase-your-project repository.

Q2. Is it required to post tweet everyday?

A. You need to tweet your progress everyday with the #100DaysOfCode hashtag, but you are allowed to skip a day, your health is the most important. You can see the tweet example here: https://twitter.com/search?q=%23100DaysOfCode&src=typeahead_click

Q3. Who to join?

A. Only Filipino students & career shifters can participate to this event. If you're not Filipino and want to join us, please join discord channel of EddieHub Community and share your progress in #standup channel.

Q4. Who are allowed to get SWAG?

A. Only from the Philippines can be able to get SWAG. We can't ship internationally.

Q5. Is there any swag we can get even we don't win?

A. We will try to give some swag. Note: The SWAG are limited and it takes time to deliver. Even if you don't win, the most important is you learn, improve your coding skills, gain connections and etc.

Q6. What are the prizes for the winners?

A. We will choose the top 5 awesome projects that submitted on the showcase-your-project repository. The 5 winners will get T-shirt codes from GitHub and some SWAG. Note: Only Filipino can submit their final project to the repository.

Q7. Will I get certificate if I join?

A. Yes, you will receive certificate of participation if you complete the #100DaysOfCode challenge
